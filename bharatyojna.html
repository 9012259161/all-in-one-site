Popular Government Schemes

Detailed information about major government initiatives

PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi

Agriculture Launched: 2019

Income support scheme providing ₹6,000 per year to small and marginal farmer families.

Official Website

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY)

Health Launched: 2018

World's largest health insurance scheme providing coverage of ₹5 lakh per family per year.

Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Housing Launched: 2015

Housing for all by 2022 with affordable housing for urban and rural poor.

Official Website

Mukhyamantri Kanya Sumangala Yojana (UP)

Women & Child Launched: 2019

Uttar Pradesh scheme providing financial assistance for girl child education and welfare.

Official Website

PM Mudra Yojana

Business Launched: 2015

Provides loans up to ₹10 lakh to non-corporate, non-farm small/micro enterprises.

Official Website

Provides affordable working capital loans to street vendors for livelihood support.
